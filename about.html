Who Am I?

My name is Shatat Al Hamid. I am a passionate freelance graphic designer on a mission to transform unique concepts and ideas into extraordinary visual elements and finalized products. I mainly work with The Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Procreate, and many other design software to create and develop any Graphic or UX Design projects I work on.

With my artistic vision and a keen eye for detail, I work with clients to breathe life into brands, logos, and marketing materials, crafting captivating designs that demand attention. My client process includes 5-10 sketches and ideation pieces, 3-5 mockups, and finally, a beautiful finished piece!

If you would like to contact me, please go to the Contact tab above! Thanks for dropping by!
